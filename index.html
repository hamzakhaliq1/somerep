ClubWise2 - Reset Your Password

This guide provides a straightforward process for resetting your ClubWise password, ensuring you regain access quickly and securely. It outlines the necessary steps, including answering security questions and updating your password. Additionally, it offers support options if you're unsure about your answers, making it a comprehensive resource for users needing assistance. Edited 9.1.26



1. Navigate to the ClubWise login page

2. Click "Forgotten password?"

3. Complete all security question answers

4. Input and confirm your new password

5. Click 'set password'

6. Once updated you will see a completion screen

7. If you are unsure of the answers to your security questions, please contact ClubWise Support. We can assist you in resetting them, provided we receive written authorisation from your Franchisee, Owner or Manager.

To get in touch with us, please submit a request via our online support form:https://support.clubwise.com/s/clientsupport

Tip: Tip! If you have a Bureau Login for multi club access - please reset your login and contact ClubSupport to request a replication to your group of clubs.
